How it works

Three steps to your strongest application.

A focused, proven process built around your individual profile and goals.

Step 01

Free diagnostic

We start by understanding exactly where you stand. We review your GPA, coursework, activities, and test scores and give you an honest, campus-by-campus read on your chances — before you spend a dollar.

Step 02

Strengths and strategy

We identify your unique strengths and build a strategy around them — campus list, major selection, PIQ angles, and activities framing — all aligned to your short and long-term goals to maximize every part of your application.

Step 03

Build, refine, submit

We work through every component of your UC application with you — drafting and refining your PIQs, polishing your activities section, and doing a full review before you hit submit so nothing is left to chance.
